इस योजना के बारे में · About

सुकन्या समृद्धि योजना 10 साल से कम उम्र की बेटियों के लिए है। Open a savings account at any post office or bank in your daughter's name. Deposit ₹250–₹1,50,000 per year. Account matures when she turns 21.

मुख्य फायदे · Key Benefits

  • 8.2% tax-free interest
    Highest rate among all small savings schemes in India
  • धारा 80C के तहत टैक्स छूट
    Up to ₹1.5 lakh deduction under Section 80C per year
  • Partial withdrawal at 18
    Up to 50% for daughter's education after age 18

कौन आवेदन कर सकता है · Eligibility

  • Girl child below 10 years of age
  • Account opened by parent or legal guardian
  • Must be a resident of India
  • Max 2 accounts per family (one per girl child)
  • NRI families not eligible after becoming non-resident
